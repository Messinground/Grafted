Card Locker

This is where your progress pays up!

Each day that you complete all the tasks that were due, you gain +1 Momentum. If you leave 3 or more incomplete, you instead lose 1 Momentum.

Momentum affects your odds of unlocking a card each day. Start with 0 and adjust the slider below according to your progress. You can have anywhere between -10 and +10 momentum at a time. This way if you have to take an extended break for any reason - you still have a chance of earning a card.

Once you've set your Momentum on the slider, Roll the dice and see whether you can surpass the threshold to discover something new! This number will change each day - so it's important to check in often to see what your odds are looking like.

As you get new cards, save your collection to a folder somewhere you won't lose it and print them off as needed. But remember - you only have as many copies of a card as you've unlocked! If you've only unlocked a card once, you'll only be allowed to use one copy of it in your deck.
